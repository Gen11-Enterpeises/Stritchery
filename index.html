FAQs

Questions
& Answers

Have questions about our services? We've answered the most common ones below. For anything else, reach out directly.

✦ Ask Us Anything
Do you accept bulk uniform orders for schools?
+
Yes, absolutely. We specialize in bulk orders for schools, colleges and institutions. Our team manages orders from 50 to 50,000+ pieces with consistent quality and competitive pricing. Dedicated account managers are assigned for institutional clients.
What is the minimum order quantity?
+
Our minimum order quantity is 25 pieces for bulk orders. For custom tailoring and individual pieces, we have no minimum — each order receives the same meticulous attention regardless of size.
How long does an order typically take?
+
Standard turnaround is 10–15 working days for bulk orders and 5–7 working days for custom pieces. Rush orders can be accommodated with prior arrangement. We provide real-time order tracking for all clients.
Can you match our institution's specific colours and logo?
+
Yes! We offer colour-matched fabrics using Pantone and RAL colour systems. Logo embroidery and screen-printing services are also available for institutional branding across all uniform types.
Do you provide fabric samples before ordering?
+
Absolutely. We provide fabric swatch samples and trial garments before bulk production begins. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the fabric quality, colour, and fit before we proceed with the full order.
What fabrics do you work with?
+
We work with a curated range including premium cotton, polyester-cotton blends, wool, linen, and technical performance fabrics. All fabrics are sourced from certified mills and meet IS/ISO quality standards.
Is there a warranty or quality guarantee?
+
Every Stitchery garment comes with a 6-month quality guarantee. If any stitching defect or fabric issue arises within this period under normal wear, we repair or replace at no additional cost.
